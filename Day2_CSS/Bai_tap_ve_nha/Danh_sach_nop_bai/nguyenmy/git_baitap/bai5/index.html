FIFA World Cup 2014 News

The biggest scoreline in the history of the FIFA qualifiers - and indded in indeed in the history of internation football - was recorded on 11 April 2001,When Autralia beat American Samoa 31 -0.

This legendary match also brought global renown for Archie Thompson, whose 13 golbal haul set a new world record, which stands to this day, for an individual player in a single international match.

And though the defeat earned American Samoa ignominy, so inspiring has their subsequent recovery been that it is now the subject of an acclaimed documentary, ‘Next Goal Wins’, showing across the world.
