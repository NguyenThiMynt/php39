Ingredients

  • 6 Eggs

  • 2 Tablespoons of vanilla extract
  • 2 Tealespoons of baking powder
  • 1 Cup of sugar

Instruction

  1. Add eggs, flour and vanilla extract and mix
  2. Combine with sugar and baking power and stir
  3. Eat half the cookie batter because it is delicious
  4. Take the rest of the cookie batter and make cookie
  5. Bake at 350 for 20 minutes
